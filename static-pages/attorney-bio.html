Attorney

Kelvin L. Roquemore

Co-Founder & Managing Partner

(972) 325-6591 kelvin@roqski.com

Over 25 years of experience in Texas state and federal courtrooms.

Kelvin L. Roquemore serves as co-founder and managing partner at Roquemore Skierski PLLC, specializing in commercial litigation. He brings over 25 years of experience in Texas state and federal courtrooms.

His work centers on disputes affecting owner-operated enterprises and business founders, particularly involving partnership conflicts, contractual breaches, fraudulent conduct, and fiduciary violations.

When litigation becomes necessary, Roquemore develops strategic approaches aligned with client objectives. He handles high-stakes, high-pressure cases with focus on outcomes. The firm frequently engages him for significant matters requiring emergency relief — safeguarding shareholder interests, protecting proprietary information and client rosters, preventing unlawful asset transfers, and securing collateral possession.

His background encompasses commercial litigation at an AM 200 national firm, senior counsel responsibilities at a Fortune 500 corporation, and commercial lending experience. This foundation shapes his evaluation of dispute leverage involving contractual terms, financial documentation, collateral, ownership records, and operational realities of private enterprises.
