Practice

Counsel for companies, founders, and executives — across the lifecycle of a business.

Roquemore Skierski PLLC represents clients in commercial matters from formation and transactions through litigation and recovery. Our work concentrates in Texas state and federal courts; our reach is statewide.

How we work

A team-based approach,
built for speed and depth.

Every engagement is staffed and managed the same way — whether the matter is a closing or a courthouse. The structure is what lets us move quickly without sacrificing rigor.

01

Full team on every matter.

A partner, an attorney, and a paralegal staff each engagement. Urgent issues get same-day attention; routine work moves without bottlenecking the partner.

02

Decades in Texas courts.

120+ combined years practicing in state and federal courts across Dallas, Fort Worth, Collin County, and surrounding jurisdictions. We know the venues, procedural rules, and the unwritten ones.

03

Built on modern infrastructure.

Case management, e-discovery, and communications systems set up so we can move on TROs, injunctions, and settlement windows without operational delay.
