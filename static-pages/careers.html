Careers

Join a team that wins through preparation, collaboration,
and relentless advocacy.

Roquemore Skierski PLLC represents businesses, entrepreneurs, and executives in business law and commercial litigation across Texas.

Inside the firm you will find open doors, shared knowledge, and mutual respect — senior attorneys work side by side with associates and paralegals on every matter, and we hire people who care about the work, the client, and each other.

Our Standard

Hard work is our standard. Being the most prepared in the room is our hallmark.

Values

What the work asks of us.

Three principles guide how we staff matters, mentor associates, and serve clients. They are how we evaluate ourselves, and how we evaluate the people we hire.

01

Client-First Advocacy

Every matter answers to the client's business objective. Strategy, staffing, and execution are all built around it — not the other way around.

02

Collaboration

Complex matters demand teamwork. Partners, attorneys, and paralegals work cases together across practice areas and experience levels — never in silos.

03

Growth

Professional development is part of the job, not an afterthought. Mentorship from senior partners, CLE support, and a clear path upward.
