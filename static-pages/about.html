About Roquemore
Skierski PLLC

A business law and commercial litigation firm serving Dallas and Fort Worth.

Founders Kelvin Roquemore and Doug Skierski have decades of trial experience in business and commercial litigation across Texas state and federal courts.

The firm advises clients on structuring transactions, buying and selling businesses or real estate, business formation, corporate governance, and conducting due diligence. We also represent clients in partnership and shareholder disputes, contract breaches, and business torts — big-law expertise with boutique care. We serve clients in and around Dallas, Fort Worth, Frisco, McKinney, Plano, and the surrounding area.

A firm built by trial lawyers, for businesses that need one.

Roquemore Skierski PLLC was founded on the conviction that complex commercial matters deserve the full attention of senior counsel — not delegation down the chain.

Kelvin Roquemore and Doug Skierski met as commercial litigators in Dallas, where they spent the better part of two decades trying business disputes in Texas state and federal courts. Both had built their careers inside larger firms; both had watched the same pattern repeat — sophisticated clients paying for senior partners and getting work product from the most junior attorney on the team.

They started Roquemore Skierski PLLC in 2018 to do it differently. Every matter is staffed with a partner, an associate, and a paralegal from day one. The lawyers who quote a strategy are the same ones who execute it. Cases get tried by the people who understand them.

The firm has grown deliberately. New attorneys are added when the practice can support them with real mentorship and meaningful caseloads, not when the marketing calendar calls for a press release. The result is a practice that handles high-stakes matters with the depth of a much larger firm and the responsiveness of a much smaller one.

Today the firm serves owner-managed businesses, executives, and entrepreneurs across Dallas-Fort Worth and the broader Texas market — with offices in Dallas, Fort Worth, McKinney, and Mansfield, and a track record built one matter at a time.

Our Process

01
Case Assessment & Strategy

We review the governing documents, build a factual timeline, gather key evidence, confirm deadlines, and align the legal approach with the client's business objectives.

02
Demand & Early Protective Action

We send a strategic demand letter identifying the dispute, proposing solutions, and setting a deadline. When assets or trade secrets are at risk, we seek immediate court relief to prevent further harm.

03
Negotiation & Case Development

We pursue negotiation or mediation where productive, while continuing to preserve evidence and develop the case so leverage remains intact if settlement efforts do not succeed.

04
Litigation, Trial & Enforcement

If a resolution is not reached, we file suit, use focused motions and discovery to narrow the dispute, present the case at trial, and enforce any favorable judgment.

Our Mission

To help businesses navigate litigation risk and opportunity by providing exceptional legal counsel driven by seasoned judgement, strategic experience, and modern legal technology.

That means pairing decades of courtroom experience with modern practice tools, and staffing every engagement with attorneys who know the business context behind the dispute — so our clients get counsel that is both sharper and more practical than a bigger firm could deliver.

Our Vision

If we do not believe we can create value for our clients in providing legal services, we will not take the matter.

If we feel a client can be better served by another attorney — or if some of what they need can be better provided by a non-attorney — we will tell them. Whether the work is resolving a dispute, building a business, or protecting a family or business legacy, the principal aim of our services is to add value.

Our Team

A reputation for hard work and integrity. Since the firm's inception, we have built a track record of big results for our clients — and of getting them with honesty and professionalism.
