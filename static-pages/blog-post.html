Partnership Disputes

The Top 5 Partnership Dispute Law Firms in Dallas.

Kelvin Roquemore February 25, 2026 8 min read

Selecting the right law firm for a partnership dispute in Dallas significantly impacts both case trajectory and business operations.

These disputes demand swift action, relying on partnership agreements, accurate records, and immediate steps to protect business control, assets, and customer relationships.

Roquemore Skierski PLLC compiled this list to help business owners evaluate Dallas firms handling partnership disputes and related litigation. Selections reflect publicly available information about practice focus, dispute resolution experience, and professional recognition.

Disputes may involve partnership deadlock, fund diversion, embezzlement, management disagreements, or contested buyouts. The firms below provide established representation in this litigation category.

1. Roquemore Skierski

The litigation team, led by co-founders Kelvin Roquemore and Doug Skierski, represents owner-managed businesses and entrepreneurs in state and federal courts.

With Roquemore’s 25+ years in business litigation and Skierski’s background in litigation, mediation, and arbitration, the firm prioritizes control, continuity, and equitable resolution.

Partnership dispute practice encompasses:

  • Breaches of partnership or operating agreements
  • Ownership and management deadlock
  • Company fund misuse
  • Confidentiality and competition issues

The firm publishes case outcomes and reports substantial recoveries in contract breaches, partnership disputes, and commercial fraud matters.

2. Sul Lee Law

Managed by founder Sul Lee and managing attorney Diren Singhe, this Dallas-based litigation team represents businesses in commercial disputes. Multiple senior attorneys and a transactional lead handle disputes from business operations, contracts, and cross-border activity.

Recognized in Chambers USA’s Spotlight Guide for General Commercial Litigation, the firm addresses disagreements regarding contract terms, management authority, fund use, and fiduciary duty breaches. Services include analyzing governing agreements, negotiating buyouts, restructuring, and representing clients in mediation, arbitration, or court.

3. The Hunnicutt Law Group

Led by founding attorney J. Stephen Hunnicutt (licensed since 1989), the team brings courtroom and alternative dispute resolution experience. Prior roles included in-house counsel for a Fortune 500 energy company.

The firm addresses common partnership fractures:

  • Contract breaches
  • Company direction disputes
  • Management decision disagreements
  • Ownership expectation mismatches
  • Asset diversion or misuse
  • Confidentiality issues

Emphasis centers on early, private resolution through negotiation, mediation, or arbitration, with litigation as fallback when negotiation fails.

4. Law Offices of Brad Jackson

Led by Brad Jackson and Cheryl L. Mann, this team represents business owners in shareholder and partner litigation across North Texas. Jackson holds board certification in Civil Trial Law from the Texas Board of Legal Specialization, with 50+ combined firm experience.

The firm pursues and defends claims from broken business relationships:

  • Breach of contract
  • Ownership and control disputes
  • Breach of fiduciary duty

Notable results include affirming a trial-court summary judgment in a reported $1 million partnership dispute appeal.

5. Mathur Law Offices

Led by attorney Sanjay Mathur (practicing since 1995), this firm represents Dallas-area business owners in disputes involving management disagreements, financial issues, alleged contract breaches, and partnership dissolution in state and federal court.

The firm emphasizes minimal-disruption resolution through partnership agreement review, business record analysis, negotiation or mediation, and litigation when necessary.

Mathur has been recognized as a SuperLawyer since 2021, named a Top 100 Lawyer by The National Advocates, and holds a 5.0 Avvo client-review rating.
