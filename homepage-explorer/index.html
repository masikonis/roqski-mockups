Roquemore Skierski represents businesses, entrepreneurs, and executives in high-stakes commercial litigation across Texas. The firm staffs a dedicated legal team on every matter, from initial strategy through trial. Business litigation is not a side practice here. It is the entire focus.

Practice Areas

  • When a business relationship breaks down and the stakes are significant, the response needs to be strategic, not reactive. The firm handles complex commercial disputes in state and federal courts across Texas, from breach of contract claims to multi-party business torts.

    Learn more

  • A vendor stops performing. A partner walks away from their obligations. A buyer defaults on a purchase agreement. Contract breaches put operations at risk and demand a clear legal strategy to recover what is owed and protect the business going forward.

    Learn more

  • Partners, officers, and advisors owe legal duties to the businesses and people who trust them. When those duties are violated through self-dealing, misuse of funds, or disloyal conduct, the firm pursues accountability and recovery through litigation.

    Learn more

  • Fraud in a business context ranges from misrepresentation in a deal to systematic diversion of company assets. The firm brings claims for actual and exemplary damages and, where necessary, seeks emergency relief to prevent further harm while the case proceeds.

    Learn more

  • Before a dispute begins, the contract defines the relationship. The firm reviews and drafts commercial agreements, purchase contracts, and operating documents to protect clients before problems arise, not after.

    Learn more

  • Some situations require immediate court intervention. The firm obtains and defends temporary restraining orders and injunctions when ongoing harm demands a faster resolution than standard litigation timelines allow.

    Learn more

  • When business partners disagree on direction, distributions, or management, the dispute can threaten the company itself. The firm handles partner buyouts, dissolution actions, and claims arising from breach of partnership agreements.

    Learn more

  • Minority shareholder oppression, mismanagement of corporate assets, and disputes over company control require litigation that understands both corporate governance and courtroom strategy. The firm represents shareholders and corporations in these matters.

    Learn more
